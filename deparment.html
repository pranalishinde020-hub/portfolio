Electronic and Telecommunication

Electronic engineering is a sub-discipline of electrical engineering focusing on the design, development, and testing of active electronic components, semiconductor devices, and integrated circuits. It powers modern technology.

Electrical engineering

Electrical engineering is a core engineering discipline focused on the study, design, and application of systems that use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. It powers modern society by dealing with everything from massive power grids to microchips.

civil engineering

Civil engineering is a core professional discipline that designs, constructs, and maintains the physical and naturally built environment. It is the backbone of modern society, responsible for vital infrastructure like roads, bridges, water supply systems, and public facilities.
